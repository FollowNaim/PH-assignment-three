01.

Initial Consultation

Connect with us to discuss your vision, needs, and preferences. We’ll review your ideas and provide insights on how we can bring your dream barn house to life.

  • Personalized Design Discussion

  • Custom Needs Assessment

  • Expert Guidance on Vision and Goals

02.

Design Proposal

Receive a customized design proposal based on our consultation. This includes concept sketches, floor plans, and a detailed project overview to ensure we’re on the same page.
